Classic Butter Chicken with Basmati Rice

By: Nicolas Sanchez & Krishma Patel

Ingredients for the Chicken Marinade:

6 oz Chicken Breast, 3 tsp Plain Greek Yogurt, 1 tsp Turmeric, 1 tsp Chili Powder, 1 tsp Kashmiri Chili Powder, 2 tbsp Ginger Paste, 2 tbsp Garlic Paste

Ingredients for the Curry:

8 Roma Tomatoes, cubed, 1 Yellow Onion, diced, 1 tbsp Ginger Paste, 1 tbsp Garam Masala, 2 tsp Turmeric, 1 tsp Cumin Seeds, 1 tsp Chili Powder, 1 tbsp Kashmiri Chili Powder, Salt, to taste, 4 tbsp Cooking Oil, 1 cup Heavy Whipping Cream

Ingredients for the Basmati Rice:

1/2 cup Basmati Rice, 3/4 cup Water, 1 tsp Cumin Seeds, Salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Combine chicken with yogurt, turmeric, chili powders, ginger paste, and garlic paste. Allow marinating for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Cook the Chicken: Sear the marinated chicken in a pan until fully cooked, about 5-10 minutes. Set aside.

  3. Prepare the Rice: Rinse and soak the basmati rice for 20-30 minutes. In a pot, add water, cumin seeds, and salt. Bring to a boil, then add the rice. Reduce heat to medium-low and cook for 10-15 minutes. Let it sit for a few minutes to absorb excess water.

  4. Make the Curry Base: In your chosen cookware, heat oil and sweat the diced onions. Add ginger paste, garlic paste, and the spices. Then, incorporate the cubed tomatoes. Cook until the mixture becomes fragrant and the tomatoes soften.

  5. Blend and Simmer: Let the mixture cool slightly, then blend it with heavy whipping cream. Return to the cookware, add the cooked chicken, and simmer for 10-15 minutes. Adjust seasonings to taste.

  6. Serve: Plate the butter chicken with the fluffy basmati rice and some warm, buttered naan.
