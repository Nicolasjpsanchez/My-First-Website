Classic Butter Chicken with Basmati Rice

Ingredients

Chicken Marinade

  • 6 oz Chicken Breast
  • 3 tsp Plain Greek Yogurt
  • 1 tsp Turmeric
  • 1 tsp Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri Chili Powder
  • 2 tbsp Ginger Paste
  • 2 tbsp Garlic Paste

Ingredients for the Curry

  • 8 Roma Tomatoes, cubed
  • 1 Yellow Onion, diced
  • 1 tbsp Ginger Paste
  • 1 tbsp Garam Masala
  • 2 tsp Turmeric
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tsp Chili Powder
  • 1 tbsp Kashmiri Chili Powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 4 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 1 cup Heavy Whipping Cream

Ingredients for the Basmati Rice

  • 1/2 cup Basmati Rice
  • 3/4 cup Water
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    • Combine chicken with yogurt, turmeric, chili powders, ginger paste, and garlic paste.
    • Allow to marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the Chicken:
    • Sear the marinated chicken in a pan until fully cooked, about 5-10 minutes.
    • Set aside.
  3. Prepare the Rice:
    • Rinse and soak the basmati rice for 20-30 minutes.
    • In a pot, add water, cumin seeds, and salt. Bring to a boil.
    • Add the rice, reduce heat to medium-low and cook for 10-15 minutes.
    • Let it sit for a few minutes to absorb excess water.
  4. Make the Curry Base:
    • Heat oil in your chosen cookware and sweat the diced onions.
    • Add ginger paste, garlic paste, and the spices.
    • Incorporate the cubed tomatoes and cook until fragrant and softened.
  5. Blend and Simmer:
    • Let the mixture cool slightly, then blend with heavy whipping cream.
    • Return to the cookware, add the cooked chicken, and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
    • Adjust seasonings to taste.
  6. Serve:
    • Plate the butter chicken with fluffy basmati rice.
    • Accompany with warm, buttered naan for a perfect meal.
